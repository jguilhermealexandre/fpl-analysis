üîç Key Metrics Explained

What the metrics mean and why they matter:

Form Rating:

  • FPL's official form score (weighted average of recent points)
  • 6.0+ = Excellent (strong hold), 4.5+ = Good, 3.0- = Poor, 1.5- = Critical
  • Short-term performance indicator

xGI (Expected Goal Involvements):

  • xG + xA = total attacking threat
  • More predictive than actual goals/assists
  • High xGI with low returns = player is DUE for hauls
  • 71% accuracy predicting returns over 4-gameweek samples

Average Minutes (Last 4):

  • 75+ mins = Nailed starter (bonus: -10 points)
  • 60-75 mins = Rotation risk emerging (+20 points)
  • Below 60 = Severe rotation concerns (+35 points)
  • 60-minute threshold critical for clean sheet points

Ownership %:

  • Below 2%: High template opportunity cost (+10 points)
  • Above 40%: Less differential value (+5 points)
  • Effective ownership = your ownership vs top 10k average
